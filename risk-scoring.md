# Risk Scoring

Risk scoring assigns a probability of fraud to a transaction.

## Inputs

- user behavior
- transaction history
- device signals
- location patterns

---

## Output

A score (e.g. 0 to 1 or 0 to 100)

---

## Role

Helps detect unknown or evolving fraud patterns.

---

## Trade-Off

More sensitivity increases detection but also increases false positives.
